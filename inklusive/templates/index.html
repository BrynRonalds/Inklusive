Finding the right tattoo artist can be a daunting process. Even more daunting can be the prospect of entering a tattoo studio and feeling unwelcome or disrespected. This is especially the case for women and non-binary people. Inklusive has compiled a list of Montreal's most inclusive and welcoming studios, based on recommendations and reviews. From these studios we have compiled a database of the artists' previous work. You upload the tattoo image, we hook you up!
